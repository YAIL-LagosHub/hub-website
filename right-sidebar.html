Projects & Impact

Community-driven AI solutions advancing UN SDGs in Lagos

Current Projects

Our active projects demonstrate how AI can address real community challenges when developed through participatory approaches.

Climate Resilience AI

Active

Challenge: Lagos communities need better early warning systems for flooding and extreme weather events.

Solution: Community-trained AI models that analyze local weather patterns, drainage data, and community observations to provide hyperlocal flood predictions.

Impact: Serving 3 pilot communities with 72-hour flood warnings, enabling proactive evacuation and resource mobilization.

SDG 13: Climate Action SDG 11: Sustainable Cities

EduTech for All

Active

Challenge: Educational inequality and limited access to quality learning resources in underserved communities.

Solution: AI-powered adaptive learning platform that works offline and adapts to local languages and cultural contexts.

Impact: Deployed in 5 schools, reaching 200+ students with personalized learning experiences in English and Yoruba.

SDG 4: Quality Education SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities

Community Health AI

In Development

Challenge: Limited access to healthcare diagnostics in remote and underserved areas.

Solution: Mobile AI diagnostic tools that can be operated by community health workers with minimal training.

Progress: Prototype testing with 10 community health workers across 3 local government areas.

SDG 3: Good Health SDG 1: No Poverty

Research & Publications
