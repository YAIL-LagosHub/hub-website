Mission & Vision

Empowering youth to drive ethical AI innovation for sustainable development

Our Mission

The AI for Good Young Leaders Hub (Lagos) exists to co-create ethical AI solutions with local communities that address real-world challenges and advance the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). We empower young changemakers, innovators, researchers, entrepreneurs, and advocates to channel the power of artificial intelligence toward achieving sustainable development through community-centered innovation.

Our mission is rooted in the principle that AI should be developed with communities, not for them. Every project begins by listening to community voices, understanding local priorities, and ensuring that technological solutions reflect lived experiences and cultural contexts.

Our Vision

We envision a future where ethical AI innovation is accessible, inclusive, and community-driven across Lagos and West Africa. Our vision is of thriving local ecosystems where young leaders collaborate with communities to design, develop, and deploy AI solutions that:

  • Reflect community priorities and address locally-defined challenges
  • Advance sustainable development aligned with UN SDGs
  • Promote equity and inclusion in technology access and benefits
  • Build local capacity for ethical AI governance and innovation
  • Create measurable social impact that improves lives and livelihoods

Core Values

Community-Centered

We prioritize community voice in every stage of our work, from problem discovery through design, piloting, and deployment.

Ethical & Responsible

We champion AI that is transparent, fair, inclusive, and respects human rights and dignity.

Collaborative

We believe in the power of partnerships between youth, communities, academia, and organizations.

Sustainable

We design solutions for long-term impact, building local capacity and pathways to scale.

UN SDG Alignment

Our work is explicitly aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, with particular focus on:

SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being - AI solutions for healthcare access and health system strengthening
SDG 4: Quality Education - Educational technology and capacity building programs
SDG 5: Gender Equality - Promoting women's participation in AI and addressing gender bias
SDG 13: Climate Action - AI for climate resilience and environmental monitoring
SDG 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions - Ethical AI governance and transparency

Our Community-First Approach

We believe that meaningful AI innovation must start with community engagement. Our approach includes:

  1. Listen First: Participatory problem discovery with community members
  2. Co-Design: Collaborative solution design that reflects local knowledge
  3. Build Together: Community involvement in development and testing
  4. Deploy Responsibly: Ethical deployment with ongoing community feedback
  5. Sustain Impact: Building local capacity for long-term ownership

Ethical AI Framework

Our commitment to ethical AI is guided by principles of:

Transparency

Clear communication about how AI systems work and make decisions

Fairness

Ensuring AI systems do not perpetuate or amplify existing biases

Accountability

Clear responsibility for AI system outcomes and impacts

Privacy

Protecting individual and community data rights

Youth Leadership Development

We provide pathways for young people to develop as ethical AI leaders through:

  • Technical Skills: AI/ML training, data science, and software development
  • Leadership Roles: Hub leadership, project management, and community coordination
  • Mentorship: Access to experienced practitioners and researchers
  • Global Exposure: Participation in international forums and UN events
  • Project Experience: Real-world AI project implementation
