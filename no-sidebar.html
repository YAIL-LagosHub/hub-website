Start a Student Chapter

Bring AI for Good to your university campus and connect with our global network of young leaders.

What is a Student Chapter?

Student chapters are university-based groups that connect to our hub for mentorship, resources, and project collaboration. Each chapter operates with local autonomy while benefiting from our global network and expertise.

Chapter Structure:

  • Chapter Lead: Primary contact and local organizer
  • Technical Representative: Leads project teams and technical initiatives
  • Outreach Representative: Handles campus promotion and external relations
  • Faculty Advisor: Academic sponsor and risk oversight
  • Project Teams: Small groups (3-6 members) working on specific initiatives

Chapter Benefits

  • Access to AI for Good project templates and methodologies
  • Mentorship from hub leadership and technical experts
  • Quarterly showcase opportunities with partners
  • Connection to global network of 103 hubs
  • Training resources and workshop materials
  • Funding support for promising projects

Requirements:

  • Minimum 5 committed student members
  • Faculty advisor agreement
  • Monthly progress reports to hub
  • Participation in quarterly showcases
  • Commitment to ethical AI principles
